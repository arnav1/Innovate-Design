Innovate Designs is a venture by two students of Civil Engineering Department of IIT Madras.
Innovate Designs-Graphic Studio is an extension to the originally established Innovate Designs-Architecture Studio.
The Graphic Studio, headed by Rathees Panneer Selvan, currently takes on tasks which include Logo Designing, Brochure Designing, Visiting Cards, Company Profile and ONLINE PRINTING SERVICE.
Since we ourselves started as students, we give high priority to student start-ups and have a lot of offers for them.
The machine force that we have include Workstations and Asus Rog Series. Now forget all your printing needs, we are here to do it all.
